
WELCOME to a perilous swamp adventure. 

You are one of the three blind mice, and
you have lost your way in Shrek's swamp! You must navigate your way through the
swamp, defeating the many puzzling challenges and creatures that reside there,
to first find your cheese, and then escape from the swamp.

Don't forget that Shrek is quite territorial, and won't take kindly to visitors
in his swamp.

Remember... you are blind... use all of your senses!
